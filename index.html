Communication techniques

  1. Appropriately handle customers' confidential and private materials
    • Located on a computer, desktop, printer, etc.
  2. Appropriately deal with difficult customers or situations
    • Avoid being judgemental
    • Avoid dismissing customer issues
    • Clarify customer statements
      • Ask open ended questions to narrow scope of issue
      • Restate issues
      • Question to verify understanding
    • Do not argue with customer and/or be defensive
    • Use discretion and professionalism when discussing experiences and encounters
  3. Actively listen and avoid interrupting customer
  4. Avoid distractions
    • Personal calls
    • Personal interruptions
    • Texting/social media sites
  5. Be on time
  6. Be culturally sensitive
    • Use appropriate professional titles and designations,when applicable
  7. Maintain a positive attitude/project confidence
  8. Persent a professional appearance and wear appropriate attire
    • Match required attire of given environment
      • Business casual
      • Formal
  9. Set and meet expectations/timeline and communicate status with customer
    • Offer repair/replacement options, as needed
    • Provide proper Documentation on services provided
    • Follow up with customer/user at later date to verify satisfaction
  10. Use proper language and avoid jargon, acronyms, and slang
